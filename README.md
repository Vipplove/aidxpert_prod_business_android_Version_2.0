## Overview

**Aidxpert Business App** is a comprehensive healthcare service management platform designed for medical service providers. It enables seamless **registration, onboarding, management, and operations** for multiple healthcare business roles under one unified system.

The app works alongside the **Aidxpert Doctor App** and **Aidxpert Patient App** to create a complete, integrated digital healthcare ecosystem.
